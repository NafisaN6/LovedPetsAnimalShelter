Our Philosophy

At Loved Pets Animal Rescue, we believe all animals deserve a chance to find a loving forever home. We provide initial veterinary care to rescued animals and work to match each companion with a loving family. Volunteers help us keep the shelter running by bathing animals, playing with pets, keeping kennels clean, and helping with administrative tasks.
